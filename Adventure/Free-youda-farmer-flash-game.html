Do you want to experience some country life, like never before? Then here is Youda Farmer! Run your farm, grow your crops and provide all shops with the supplies they need. It's up to you to keep all retailers in this little village happy with your beautiful farm products. You start off with a little farm providing the baker of his flour to make bread for the villagers.
