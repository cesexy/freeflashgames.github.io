Game Description:

Moppy must collect all the stars placed in different positions. Moppy must jump from the springs provided at the bottom to get on the ledges. Moppy dies when it gets in contact with creepers and the game is over. The player needs to cross 3 levels in order to beat the game.

Play Instructions:

Use the arrow keys to move the moppy and to collect all the stars in each level. Moppy should escape from the creepers when it is stable. If moppy is jumping on the springs the creeper can't attack the moppy. Moppy has only three chances to jump on the springs continually. Game ends when the creeper attacks the moppy.
