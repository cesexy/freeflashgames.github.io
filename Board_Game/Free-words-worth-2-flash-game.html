Game Description:

The aim of the game is to create as many words as you can from the grid of 36 letters within the given time, the longer and more complicated the words the more points you score, but be careful as you can only use a word one time per game. Stretch your vocabulary to the maximum with this brain bending game and see how much your words are worth.

Play Instructions:

Simply click on the letters in the grid and click submit, or just click again on a letter you have already selected. If you make a mistake you can click the clear button. Each word you make get an extra second of time for each letter, so a six letter word earns you an extra six seconds of play, you can earn a score multiplier plus an extra 30 seconds of play by using all the tiles on the board. Make as many words as you can!
