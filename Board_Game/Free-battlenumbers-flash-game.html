BattleNumbers is a turn based strategy game. The objective is to eliminate all your opponent's pieces by conquering them with your own. Higher pieces defeat lower ones, and these will in turn take weaker ones nearby, creating a chain of combos. The game can be played against the computer, in several levels of difficulty, or against another player in the same pc. The game also features a Challenge mode, in which different objectives are presented. These introduce turn limits, survival mode, fog and camouflaged units, neutral pieces, and targets both for defending and conquering. An Highscore system is also available, allowing player competition on a greater scale.
