Game Description:

A great time-pass solitaire card game where you have to discard all cards except Aces.

Play Instructions:

1. To start Game, click the last card from the left most Deck of cards facing down. 2. If there are two or more cards of the same suit, discard all but the highest-ranked card of that suit. 3. Repeat step 2 until there are no more pairs of cards with the same suit. Whenever there are any empty spaces, you may choose the top card of another slot to be put into the empty space. 4. Deal out the next four cards from the deck onto each slot when no more card can be trashed to moved to empty slot. 5. When the last four cards have been dealt out and if Only Aces remain in all 4 slots, you win the Game, otherwise you loose. 6. Play and score more to unlock in-Game Bonuses.
