Game Description:

Grumbo Assault is a strategy defense game where you position troop spawn points and try to protect your general. At the beginning of each level, the player receives a somewhat comical briefing of what will happen during the level. Then the player goes through the setup phase where they can place and move around troop spawn points. After troops have been placed, the level will play itself out automatically. If the general survives and all enemies are killed, the player moves on to the next level.

Play Instructions:

Place spawn points during the setup phase by clicking buttons on the interface. You can drag your general around during the setup phase of each level to position him. You can also drag spawn points around during the setup phase. Once you hit start, your troops will spawn and attempt to defend your general. You can drag the interface around and hide it with the min button if it is getting in your way. Use space bar to advance through the tutorial, and press any key at the start of each level to hide the splash screen. The in game tutorial will walk a player through the basics of playing.
