Game Description:

Ace Solitaire is a solitaire card game based on creating groups of cards that sum up to 11. A unique feature for Ace Solitaire is that all dressed cards, Jacks, Queens and Kings, have been replaced by Aces. This means that there are 16 Aces in the deck. The value of any card is based on its number and an Ace is always counted as 1. You can create a group that sums up to 11 by using 2 cards or more, so 7 + 4 = 11, and 6 + 4 + Ace also counts as 11.
