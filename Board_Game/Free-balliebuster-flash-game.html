Frantic action, reaction, matching game. Swipe your mouse across Ballies of the same color in any direction and build multiple chains of high scoring explosive fun! Ballie Buster starts out fairly easy, but to make it through the later levels and score over 1 million points, (yes only the best players will make it past 1 million) you'll need to concentrate on building longer chains of matches. You can match Ballies in any direction, including diagonally, so use that to your advantage. You don't have to wait for new Ballies to fall down either, you can keep on swiping away as quickly as you can. But don't do just random swiping, that really won't help you on the later levels. ;) Don't forget to include the bomb and clock bonus when they're available. You may want to wait until the bomb is further down the screen so it takes out more Ballies when it explodes.
