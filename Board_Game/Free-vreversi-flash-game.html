Game Description:

Flash version of the game reversi

Play Instructions:

You play against the computer. During a play, any discs of the opponent's color that are in a straight line and bounded by the disc just placed and another disc of the current player's color are turned over to the current player's color. The object of the game is to have the majority of discs turned to display your color when the last empty square is filled.
