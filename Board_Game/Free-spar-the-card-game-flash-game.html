Game Description:

Spar: The Card Game is the online version of one of the rules for the new physical card game called Spar: The Card Game. The game deck includes the same cards, but offers many different ways of playing head-to-head fighting with your friends. In this online version, you are playing the "Punch Your Face" rules! Play against the computer AI or against your friends (locally).

Play Instructions:

Players take turns choosing an attack card from their hand. Both players get the same cards. The starting attacker starts with one less card. The red arrows show you where the attack will hit. The numbers show you the damage for each hit. Arrows that line up cancel each other out, minus any differences in attack power. The player with the most damage loses.
