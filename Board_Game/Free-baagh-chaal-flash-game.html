Game Description:

Baaghchaal, which is the national game of Nepal, means Tiger's move. It is a strategic, two-player board game. One player controls four tigers and the other player controls twenty goats. The tigers 'eat' the goats while the goats attempt to block the tigers' movements.

Play Instructions:

You can play as either Goats or Tigers. Goat: 1) Click on empty position to place a goat. 2) When all the goats are in the board, drag and drop from one position to another. 3) To avoid getting eaten, make sure nearby tiger cannot jump over a goat. Tiger: 1) Drag a tiger from one position to another. 2) Try to eat goats as soon as the game begins. If tigers eat 6 goats, Tiger wins the game. If goats block all the tigers' moves, Goat wins the game.
