Play Instructions:

Get People to Guess Your Word Requires a minimum of two people to play and the more the merrier. How to Play Players take turns giving hints to their words without using the illegal words. When you are not giving hints, you are guessing other player's words. Word Giver If you are the word giver, you will be presented with a panel, displaying the word you want other players to guess and words you cannot use. In the example below, Robot is the word you want people to guess. Type hints in via the chat. The other players will see the hints on a hint panel, as well as in the chat. Guesser If you are not giving the hints, your role is to try and guess the word by typing them into the chat. If a word is guessed correctly within the time allotted both word giver and guesser receive points. First person to 50 wins. In the case of a tie, the game continues until there is a clear winner.
