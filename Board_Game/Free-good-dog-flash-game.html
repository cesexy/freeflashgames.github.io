Good Dog! is a tile matching game from Accordion Games! In Good Dog, you'll challenge your memory skills as well as your speed in matching all of the dog breeds correctly as quickly as possible! Play Instructions:

To select a tile, left click on it using a mouse or pointer. To turn the same tile over, click it again. To select a second tile, simply left click it as well. If they match, you earn 100 points and are removed! If not, you lose 5 points and the tiles are turned face down again. When all tiles are cleared, you win the game!
