Game Description:

Memory Mess is a time-limited memory game. A total of 48 cards are first showed for a few seconds, during which the player should memorize as many identical pairs as possible. When the game begins, the player should choose two cards at a time to turn them face up. If the cards match, they will be removed and the player's score will increase. There are three difficulty levels with a varying number of card types. The easiest level has 2 different types, the medium level 8, and the hardest level has a stunning amount of 24 different types to recall!
