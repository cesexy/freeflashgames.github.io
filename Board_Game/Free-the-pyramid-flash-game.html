Game Description:

The Pyramid is based on the classic card solitaire game. The aim is to clear as many card lay-ups as possible in the allotted time. The cards are placed in a Pyramid form and you have to remove them one by one before you run through your deck of cards. Succeed and you move to the next round. You can play as many rounds as you can until you run out of time or you are unable to solve the lay-up.
