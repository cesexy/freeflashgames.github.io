Play free Pick Up Strings flash game.

Primary Category: Board Game.
Secondary Categories: Puzzles.
Content Rating: Everyone.


Game Description:

Pick up all sticks by creating words from the letters attached to the sticks!

Play Instructions:

Click once with your mouse on the letters attached to the selected stick to create a valid word. Double-click anywhere to check the validity of your word. If a created word is valid then the selected stick is removed and you get points for it. The time for creating a word is limited to 30 seconds per stick. If the time expires then one of the sticks from the pile will be destroyed so that you will get 0 points for it.
