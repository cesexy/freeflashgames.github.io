Play Instructions:

Tic Tac Toe is played on a 3x3 board. Player take turns to fill one field on the board with their symbol(X or O). When a player has placed 3 of their symbols in a row/column/diagonally, they win the game. If no player has done this and the board is full it is a draw. The first turn gets assigned interchangeably. Controls: Tap on an empty space on the board to place your symbol there. Game modes: Easy/Hard: This mode allows you to play against the AI. You get to play as X while the AI gets to play as O. Versus: This move allows you to play against another human player. Player 1 plays as X, while player 2 plays as O.
