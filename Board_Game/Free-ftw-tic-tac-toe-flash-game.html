Play Instructions:

Tic Tac Toe is played on a 3x3 board. Player take turns to fill one field on the board with their symbol(X or O). When a player has placed 3 of their symbols in a row/column/diagonally, they win the game. If no player has done this and the board is full it is a draw. The first turn gets assigned interchangeably.
