Game Description:

A simple swap and match game. More points the better.

Play Instructions:

Swap-Match-Remove.. Swap two tiles on the board so that you match two or more tiles. Once you have matched two or more tiles, its removed from the board and the tiles above it takes its place and new tiles come on top. Click on two tiles to swap. You can swap either horizontally or vertically only. Each level needs predefined points to clear. its indicated on the right side of the board. Each new level increases the width and height of the board by one tile each. A new figure is also added to the tiles. Click on the submit button if you cant find any more matches and submit your score.
