Game Description:

Washout is a game where the player spins, and 6 numbers are chosen at random between 0 and 9. The player has 3 choices to guess the outcome of the 6 numbers, do they make one pair, two pair, 3 of a king, straight, etc. Points are accumulated when the player chooses correctly, more points for a better hand. A strike is received if no match occurs, 3 strikes and game over.

Click the Show Selections button, and choose the 3 outcomes on the right that the player thinks gives him the best chance to earn points, and also keep the game going. Then click spin, to see the results for those choices. Points or strikes will accumulate. 3 strikes and game over.
