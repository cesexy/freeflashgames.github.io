Game Description:

Demonstrate your precise measuring skills to fulfill the winning condition! Your task in this game is to measure 6 liters of water out of a 5-liter jar and a 7-liter jar. When the game starts, the two empty jars will be placed on the grassland next to a stream. Click and drag a jar to the indicated position next to the stream to fill it with water. After that, you may click and drag the jar to pour the water into another jar, or to empty the jar by moving it to the flowers on the left of the screen. Continue the process until one of the jars contains 6 liters of water and you will win. Your score is counted based on the amount of time you have spent as displayed at the top left corner.
