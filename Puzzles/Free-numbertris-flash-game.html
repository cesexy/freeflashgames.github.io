NumberTris is a puzzle type of game where you have to create lines of numbers that add to a given value.

Play Instructions:

The numbers fall from the top of the screen and pile down at the bottom. You can control the falling number with the keyboard to put it in a place where when combined with its new neighbours they sum a given result. If the screen gets filled with numbers you loose.
