Game Description:

Plexgao is a game of mind. A simple turn based puzzle game in which you build shapes, jump enemies, and fight for board control. Play against the 5 different Ai or play your buddy via the local Versus option. Intuitive like checkers, complex like chess.

Play Instructions:

Controls - Mouse On your turn you play one brick to the board, which is your color. The objective is to build shapes, the Cross, X, and Horizontal, Vertical, & Diagnol lines, plus any combination. Once used in a shape the piece is then locked and cannot be jumped or used in another shape. Pieces will be jumped on the opponents turn if their piece is sandwiched between the opponents pieces either horizontally or Vertically. The game is finished when the board is completely filled.
