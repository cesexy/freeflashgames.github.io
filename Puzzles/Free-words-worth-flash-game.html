Game Description:

The aim of the game is to use the grid of 36 letters to make as many words you can within the given time, the longer and more complicated the words the more points you score, but be careful as you can only use a word one time per game. Each word you make you also get a extra second of time for each letter, so a six letter word earns you an extra six seconds of play. Stretch your vocabulary to the maximum with this brain bending game and see how much your words are worth.
