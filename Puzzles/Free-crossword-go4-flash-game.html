Play Instructions:

Click on the Play button to start or if you have tried this crossword before, the Continue From Last Save button will be enabled allowing you to carry on from where you left off. On the main screen you will notice that there is one red coloured square which follows your mouse pointer over the white squares in the grid. If you click on this red square it will lock in place and turn green. This is where your answer will start. Type your answer in and the box will automatically move forward in the direction of the arrow. Use the right and down arrow keys to change direction between across and down. To save the crossword to continue at another time click the Save button. For help click the Help button and if you get really stuck, click the Show Solution button.
