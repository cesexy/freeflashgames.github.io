Game Description:

Water Man is appointed to serve drinking water to people. Basic game idea is very similar to classic Pipe Man game. In this game Water Man is appointed to server water by arranging pipe pieces to lead the water from his water tank to buckets.

Play Instructions:

Connect the pipes between the enabled valve and the bucket. Click on the pipe to select the pipe and Click again to place the pipe. The pipes are be fixed on the pillars but you can fix maximum of one pipe without any support of pillar. Once you finish connecting the pipes, the water level in the bucket starts increasing and next valve in the tank get enabled . To stop the water before overflow by Click on that valve.
