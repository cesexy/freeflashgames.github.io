Game Description:

Strategically eliminate the numbered tiles.

Play Instructions:

The goal of this game is to reduce board of tiles so that as few remain as possible. Ideally you want one tile left with a face-value of 1. Eliminate tiles by dragging them onto one another. You may only combine tiles that have consecutive numbers, for instance a 2 may be moved onto a 3 or 1, but not onto a 2 or 4. Also, tiles must be vertically, horizontally, or diagonally adjacent. The round will end automatically if you make it down to one tile; otherwise, click the "I'm done" button when you can reduce the board no further. In order to pass, you must meet minimum requirements for pieces left and board value (the face value of the pieces, the lower, the better). Every 4 rounds the number range increases and the grid size cycles.
