Game Description:

Connect sets of three or more faces to score! How many sets can you complete before time runs out?

Play Instructions:

Use your mouse to play this game. Your goal is to connect three or more of the same face. Once you complete a set of three or more faces, the set will disappear and the rest of the faces will shift over to fill in the spaces. In order to go to the next level, complete the number of sets indicated on the left above each face. To move the faces, click on two that are next to each other and they will switch places on the board. They will only switch places if one of the faces that you move completes a set, otherwise the two faces will revert back to their original positions. The more sets you complete, the more points you score!
