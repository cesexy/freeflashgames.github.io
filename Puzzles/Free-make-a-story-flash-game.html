Game Description:

This is my attempt at the Mad Lib type games, with 13 stories for now and more to come! And not only that, you can even submit your own stories and see them in the game, with your name as author and in the credits as a story writer! Make a Story uses fun prompts for words, such as "D&D style treasure", "favorite food" or "goofy verb, present tense" instead of the bland "verb", "noun" etc. And it all takes place in the critter classroom library, the first locale shown for the Critterverse!

Play Instructions:

To play, simply click "Enter the Library", where you can select your book and get a description of it. Use the arrows to peruse the shelves, and click the color-coded panels at the top to narrow it by type (also useful to see what kind of story each is on the shelves!). Once you've selected your book, simply enter your word or phrase and hit the arrow to the right to flip to the next page. Don't know what you want to enter? Hit the RANDOMIZER button to get different suggestions, or simply hit the button and the arrow to move on! The page counter at the bottom right will show you how many there are to enter, and stories range from about 5-20, depending on the story itself. Once you're done, take a look at your story!
