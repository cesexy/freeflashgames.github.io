Play Instructions:

Jump Sequence is a puzzle game, the objective is to get your orb safely from start to end gateways. You complete each level by using the available intermediary gateways, in the correct orientation and sequence. Gateways have different movement properties (triangle=static, circle=rotate, square=slide, and so on.) Each gateway displays its link partner, after teleporting this is where your orb will re-appear. Gateways can both send and receive, (the indicator displays the orb's exit direction.) Gateways sometimes add a swerve to the orb - this is also displayed in the gateway's direction indicator.
