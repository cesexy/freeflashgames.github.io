Game Description:

Connect sets of three or more butterflies to score! How many sets can you complete before time runs out?

Play Instructions:

Use your mouse to play this game. Your goal is to connect three or more butterflies with the same pattern vertically or horizontally. Once you complete a set of three or more butterflies, the set will disappear and the rest of the butterflies will shift over to fill in the spaces. To move the butterflies, click on two that are next to each other and they will switch places on the board. They will only switch places if one of the butterflies that you move completes a set, otherwise the two butterflies will revert back to their original positions. The more sets you complete within the five minute time frame, the more points you score!
