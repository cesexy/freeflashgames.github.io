Game Description:

Pear School is an addictive puzzle game similar to Goober's Lab or Bejeweled, but taken to the next level. There are two ways to make a match, by color or by shape. Other features include special pear shaped wildcards, as well as the ability to rotate the entire outer edge of the board. If you like puzzle games, you'll love this!

Play Instructions:

Switch two colored shapes by dragging one on top of another adjacent one. Any matches of shape and or color of three or more disappear, score you points, and get you closer to clearing the level. Four or more in a row gives you a special pear shaped wildcard which matches all colors and shapes. Once you have received four wildcards you have the ability to rotate the outside of the board and make all the resulting matches score for you.
