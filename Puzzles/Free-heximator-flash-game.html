Game Description:

A snatch & match tessellated tile sort of game. The more tiles you snatch and match the more points you score.

Play Instructions:

Use the grabber to snatch and match the incoming tiles. Rotate your base to arrange the captured tiles. Connect three tiles of the same color to clear those tiles. Get points based on the tiles color. You lose lives if tiles hit your base before you grab them. As your points grow the speed and variety of incoming tiles also grows. Nice To Know Converter tiles have a white symbol with a colored border. The tile converts all tiles it touches to it's color. Special tiles have a white icon and border. Connect three Clear All tiles to clear all tiles and get points for every tile. Connect three Timer tiles to slow down incoming tiles a short time. Move mouse outside game to pause. More Information
