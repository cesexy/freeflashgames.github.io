Game Description:

Godsweeper marries the classic number puzzling of minesweeper with the levelling up system of an RPG. Recruit followers to your religion by safely revealing tribes people of the same or lower level than you, with each follower you safely reveal earning you XP. Your XP is then used to level up so that you can recruit higher level followers. Featuring both single and two player game modes, compete against the clock to clear the grid in as fast a time as possible or battle your friends to recruit the most followers in turn based, strategic gameplay.

Play Instructions:

Click on tiles to try and reveal followers that are the same or a lower level than you are. Higher level followers will result in game over (single player) or awarding your opponent the XP (multiplayer) Numbered tiles indicate the combined level of followers in the surrounding eight tiles.
