Game Description:

CashMachine is a puzzle game based on the match3 concept but with a innovative idea. Each time you match 3 or more items of the same kind, they transform into another kind of item of higher value. For example, you match three silver coins, they turn into one gold coin. Then you match three gold coins and they turn into one bank note and so on...
