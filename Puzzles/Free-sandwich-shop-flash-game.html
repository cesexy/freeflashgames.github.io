Game Description:

In this game you need to run a sandwich shop and make sandwiches based on customers' orders. Use the mouse the drag the ingredients to make a sandwich. Hams and eggs must be put to the pan to be cooked first before they are ready to be put into the sandwich. Don't overcook the hams and eggs, otherwise they will be burnt and cannot be used. You need to make sandwiches quickly in order to earn enough money for the level's target, otherwise you lose.
