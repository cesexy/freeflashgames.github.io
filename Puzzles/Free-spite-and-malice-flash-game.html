Game Description:

Spite and Malice, also known as Cat and Mouse or Skip-Bo, is a card game for two players.

Play Instructions:

The object is to be the first to move all your cards on the stack on the left (your play stack) to the centre stacks. The first card on a centre stack must be an Ace and then in ascending order up to the Queen and the card may come from your hand, your 4 discard piles or your play stack. You also can play a card from your hand to 1 of the 4 discard piles. This will end your turn. Only the top card of a discard pile is available for play. The King is wild and can be used for any card.
