Game Description:

Symbolic space is a puzzle game. Use the mouse to play it. The goal is to find two identical elements. Elements are falling from the top of the screen. Click two identical elements, one after the other, and the first will move to the second, then be destroyed. On its way, the first block will also destroy all the blocks that have a common point. The game has three modes: Collecting, Survival and Timing. Each level has different objectives. Also, the game has a secret level which will be available after the completing of all modes.
