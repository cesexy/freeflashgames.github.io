Game Description:

The foundations of two rings follow suit. The sevens ascend to kings, the sixes descend to aces. At the end, two rings must be formed respectively of kings and aces.

Play Instructions:

All the sevens from the deck are arranged in the left ring and all the sixes in the right ring. These cards make up the foundations and follow suit. The sevens ascend to kings, the sixes descend to aces. The next eight cards from the deck are arranged in two horizontal lines called bracelets. Take from the bracelets all suitable cards and play them on the foundations, refilling vacancies in the bracelets, and placing unsuitable cards on the talon. If the game succeeds, the two rings will be formed respectively of kings and aces. The talon may be taken up and re-dealt once.
