Game Description:

Chances is a Yahtzee type game where the player spins, and tries to get the best score by best choosing how to play the random numbers shown.

Play Instructions:

Chances is a Yahtzee style game where the objective is to fill all the different boxes on the right with the outcomes of the spins. When the player first spins, he can keep any or all of the six numbers on the upper left by clicking on the number itself. The darkened numbers will not change for the remaining 2 spins. After 3 spins, the player must choose the box on the right that would give him the best point score, i.e. if the user accumulates 4 sixes, he could choose 6's, three of a kind, or four of a kind to get points. Note that while the user can change his mind and unclick a darkened number, once a box on the right is chosen it is final. While most of the boxes are self explanatory, the Dump-It box is to use for a disappointing set of spins. The user may use three of a kind or better for a three of a kind box, etc. The player can start by clicking "Spin", and the text on the left will lead the player through the game.
