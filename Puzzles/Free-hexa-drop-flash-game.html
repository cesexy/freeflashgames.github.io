Game Description:

Play Instructions:

Connect three or more tiles of the same color to remove them from the board. After a match, the background of the matched tiles turns white. To complete a level, you must turn the entire board white. Make matches of 6 or more tiles to create a lightning tile. Using a lightning tile in a match will trigger an explosion, turning all tiles adjacent to the matched tiles white. Making matches in quick succession earns you bonus points. After making 10 successive matches, 4 random tiles on the board turn white.
