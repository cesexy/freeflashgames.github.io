Game Description:

This is the extended version of the game PiClimber. PiClimber is a tribute to Pi (also known as 3.14). The digits of Pi form the different stages of the game, and your job is to think your way through to the last digit in the best possible way. In this version all scores are squared.

Play Instructions:

Use the arrow keys to travel to the bottom right digit. The goal of the game is to minimize your score. Each time you move your score is increased with the SQUARE of the difference of the digits you moved between. You may have to click the game once with the mouse to get the arrow keys working.
