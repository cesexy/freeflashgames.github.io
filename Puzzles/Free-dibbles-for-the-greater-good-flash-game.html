Game Description:

Help the Dibbles get their king to safety. They will carry out any order you give them, even if it means certain death. Dibbles is an updated take on Lemmings, but with our own unique and original twist. Each action costs a Dibble and you only have to save the king to progress, with a bonus for saving extra Dibbles. Instead of clicking on the Dibbles, you place command stones - the first Dibble to encounter the stone will carry out it's orders, in most cases they will remember the order until it's the right time to carry it out, meaning you can queue up commands.
