Aces Square Solitaire game: Discard all the cards in pairs of the same suit, except the four Aces.

Play Instructions:

Discard pairs of cards of the same suit (for example, 5 of hearts and a King of hearts) that are in the same row or column. Cards are automatically replaced from the stock. If the stock is exhausted, click on the Fill button to consolidate the cards. The game is won if only the four Aces remain on the tableau and the remaining forty eight lower-ranking cards have been paired up.
