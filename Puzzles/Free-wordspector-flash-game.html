Play Instructions:

The goal is to solve Mystery Words with the help of colour coded clues. Points are awarded based on the time used and guesses needed. 1) Enter a word using either the mouse or the keyboard. (Clicking a letter on the input area removes it and all following letters. Given letters can't be removed.) 2) Click Submit. The word is added to the word list enhanced with colour coded clues.
