Game Description:

Diamonds Diamonds is a logic game with goal to find all color diamonds in each level. You must be careful to click on black diamond, otherwise you loose one life.

Play Instructions:

Diamonds Diamonds is a logic game. Goal is to find all color diamonds in each level, but not black diamonds. If you click on card, it is turned and color diamond with number is displayed. Number indicates, how many color diamonds are around this card. Number 8 is the biggest, and it means that all cards around are safe - without black diamond - and you can click on them. If number is smaller, be careful, one or more of the surrounding cards contains black diamonds. Realise, that on the edge of the board, bigest number is not 8, but 5(only cards arounds are counts). And on the corners, biggest number is 3. If you will click on card with black diamond, you will lost one life. You start with 3 lives. During gameplay, you can get more lives. Game has three difficulty modes. Difficulty is increasing during playing of levels. Each color diamond give to Player different score. At the end, player can submit score to leadboard.
