Game Description:

This is a numerical puzzle maze game. The player uses the numbers on the tiles to go from start to end before they run out of moves. Each board is randomly generated so players will most likely never see the same grid twice!

Play Instructions:

Navigate the field using the up, down, left, and right keys on your keyboard. The distance you travel is equal to the tile you are standing on. You have to reach the tile marked E in 30 moves or less to win. If you run out of moves you lose. In the screen that shows if you won or lost you click the mouse to reset the board and play it again.
