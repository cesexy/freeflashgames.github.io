Test your skills with this challenging word game. Simply create words out of touching blocks. Guess the super word for more points! Create words by "chaining" letters that are touching adjacent, horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Words must contain at least three letters and no letter may be used more than once within a single word. Solve the super word (the longest word possible with the current set of letters) and advance into "Super Mode" which adds a 15X multiplier to each letter score and pauses the clock. Super Mode lasts for 10 seconds, each word found in Super Mode add 2 seconds of time per letter. Hitting the "Jumble" button will refresh the letter board and subtract 10 seconds from the clock. Pausing the game more than 3 times resets your game to prevent cheating. Use the keyboard shortcuts! Spacebar = "Jumble", Enter = "Submit"
