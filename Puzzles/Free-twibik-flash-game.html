Play Instructions:

Pick two similar shapes on same row or column. Those shapes and all other shapes between them will disappear. Clear top row to drop new row of shapes. Pay attention to level up counter, once it is cleared you are awarded with full board and new shape. If you run out of moves you have 3 lifesavers, but each lifesaver can be used only once.
