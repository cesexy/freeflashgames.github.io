Game Description:

Match 3 gameplay combined with crazy Nyan Cat - pure awesomeness :) The goal of this flash game is to get as many points in 90 seconds as possible. You swap Nyan Cat to form horizontal or vertical sets of the same color. The more Nyan Cats you can connect this way, the higher your score will be. Furthermore there are special stones as a reward if you can connect more then 3 Nyan Cats. Created by FlashGameHQ.com

Play Instructions:

Click two horizontal or vertical adjacent Nyan Cats with the left mouse button to swap their position - or just drag&drop them. The more Nyan Cats of the same color you can connect by this move, the more points you will get. Furthermore, if you can connect more then 3 - you will get a special gem bomb. The effect of this gem bomb varies depending on the number of Nyan Cats. To activate a bomb you need to connect it to at least two other of the same color. Hint: You can mute the music ;)
