Game Description:

The classic solitaire game in 3 levels: easy, casual and normal.

Play Instructions:

The object of the game is to build eight foundations up in suit from ace to king. Within the tableau, cards are built down and in suit. Spaces within the tableau may be filled with any card. Click on the stock to get a new card to play. - Normal: Only one card at a time may be moved and there is no redeal. - Casual: Only one card at a time may be moved, but you can redeal the stock. - Easy: You can move cards already in the correct sequence and you can redeal.
