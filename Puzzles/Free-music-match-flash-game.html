Game Description:

Its a memory based game where you have to remember the positions of the previously played sounds. The board consists of many cards/speakers. Each card has a sound which is played when you click it.

Play Instructions:

Click a card/speaker to play its sound. Try clicking another with the same sound playback. If you click the correct match you score 20 points and the pair of cards will be removed from the board. If you fail to match a pair you lose 5 points and you have to start again. However you can remember the previous postions of the audio which you can try to match if you find a similar sound. You have to clear the board to win the game. To get a high score try clearing the board with minimal tries.
