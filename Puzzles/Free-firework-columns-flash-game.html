Game Description:

In this game columns of 3 firework bombs will fall down from the top of the screen, and it is your job to arrange the bombs so that 3 or more bombs of the same colour form a straight line horizontally, vertically or diagonally. Play Instructions:

Arrange the firework bombs so that 3 or more bombs of the same colour form a straight line horizontally, vertically or diagonally. Use the arrows to move the falling bombs, press the up arrow to rotate the falling bombs.
