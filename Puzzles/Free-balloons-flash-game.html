Game Description:

Can you destroy all of the balloons in one go? Your target in this game is to pop the balloons with the darts. At each level you will be given a certain number of darts, as shown at the top left corner of the screen. An arrow-shaped power gauge will appear in front of the dart, and you can move your mouse to change the shooting direction, and click the gauge to set the shooting force. A dart will then be shot according to your settings, and you can continue the process until all darts available in the current level are used. Your score will then be counted based on the number of destroyed balloons and unused darts, and a bonus will be given if all balloons of the level are gone.
