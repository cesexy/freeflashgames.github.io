Game Description:

We present you a puzzle game where a player must eliminate tiles from the board by clicking on 3 of the same. By clicking tiles in a specific orders, combos are being made. Combos grant you power tiles that on activation can blast other tiles out of the board. Play to gain the highest score!

Play Instructions:

Match 3 tiles of the same color by clicking on them The player is awarded with bonus points if clicking the tiles on a specific order that triggers a combo. Combos can trigger power tiles to appear on the board and when a power tile is being marked along with other matching tiles, it activates a small explosion, destroying tiles nearby the exploded power tile . The game is over when there is no room for new tiles to appear or when the time counter runs out. The goal of this game is to get the highest score as possible. The game starts slow but increase in speed and difficulty while playing.
