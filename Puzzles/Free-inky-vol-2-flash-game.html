Play the Inky game. Fill in the blank squares so that each row and each column contain all of the digits 1 thru 9 without repetitions. The heavy lines indicate areas (called cages) that contain groups of numbers that can be combined (in any order) to produce the result shown in the cage, with the indicated math operation.
