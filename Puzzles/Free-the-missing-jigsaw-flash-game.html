Game Description:

Every enthusiast of jigsaw puzzle must know the devastating feeling of missing a few pieces when the task is almost completed. In Missing Jigsaw, your goal is to choose the correct pieces to complete the puzzles. At each level of the game, you will be given an incomplete image, while a number of pieces will be displayed under the picture. Click to select the tiles so that the whole picture can be revealed. If all of the chosen pieces are correct, a score will be given based on the amount of time you have spent as recorded on the left of the screen. If any of the selected tiles is wrong, 1000 points will be deducted, and you need to pick the correct pieces in order to proceed. As you advance in the game, the number of missing pieces will increase. Can you recover the pictures to their perfect conditions?
