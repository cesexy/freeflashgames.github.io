Balenza is a fun, innovative, physics based puzzle game. The goal of Balenza is to balance as many objects on the platform as you can without dropping one off the screen.

Play Instructions:

The goal of Balenza is to balance as many objects on the platform as you can. When a falling object is highlighted with yellow, click and hold the mouse button to pull it towards your cursor. Use the mouse to position the objects where you want them to land. When you let go of the mouse button, the object will fall. When that object touches the platform or another object youll score a point and a new one will be released. You can only control a falling object for 10 seconds, so think fast! TIP: Be gentle. The harder the falling objects hit the platform, the more it will rock.
