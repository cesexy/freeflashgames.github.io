Remove groups of the same coloured blox and try to get as a high a score as possible. Tactical brain game with an underwater theme!

Play Instructions:

The aim of the game is to select and remove blox of the same colour. To select some blox, click once. The blox will be highlighted. To remove it click the same blox again.
