Game Description:

There is a pumpkin shortage this year and thus a shortage of pumpkin pie, and, as a farmer, you decide to turn to Kweak Gureau Industries rapid growth, perpetually respawning pumpkin seeds. Pick the pumpkins, smash them, and make them into pies to make a profit before time runs out and the holiday season arrives. Beware of crows stealing your profits. Also, don't drop the pumpkins.

Play Instructions:

Use the A and D keys to move back and forth. Press SPACE to pick up ripe pumpkins (dark orange). Toss them into the pumpkin masher on the left by pressing SPACE again. When the pumpkin masher has 5 pumpkins in it, it will squish the pumpkins into a bucket. Pick up the bucket with SPACE and toss it with SPACE again into the pie making oven on the right side of screen. A successful pie make will earn $7. Dropping a pumpkin will lose $2. A crow stealing a pumpkin loses $5. Throwing a pumpkin into the smasher when its full has no penalty. Make those pies!
