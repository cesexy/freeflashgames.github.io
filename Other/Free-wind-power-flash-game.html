Game Description:

Wind Power is a logic game with realistic physics. You must touch every color block with a ball of the same color, but a ball may be moved only with fans blowing on it.The task is to put fans properly and do it as fast as possible. The game contains 15 levels with different difficulty.

Play Instructions:

Drag fans from the panel on the left and attach it to hooks in the level. Rotate them left by holding ctrl while click , rotate right with shift + click. You must put fans to affect falling balls, so that they touch blocks with the same color. All the colourful blocks in the level must be touched by correct balls (and no others) to win.
