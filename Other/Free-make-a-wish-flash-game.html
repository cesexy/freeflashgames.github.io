Before you start Solitaire - make a wish. From how correctly and properly it will be compiled, the final result depends. Specify it for yourself and keep in mind all the time until the solitaire is completed.

Play Instructions:

To start a few times shuffle the deck. After distribution in the lower right corner there is an open card, which can be moved (if it is not ace). Aces are not moved. Soliter is over and the result will automatically appear as soon as in right-hand column would be four aces.
