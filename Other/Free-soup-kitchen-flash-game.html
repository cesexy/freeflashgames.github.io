Game Description:

You're working for a nearby homeless shelter that is in need of people like you. You are needed to cook certain recipes and to serve them to the poor. Oh and by the way, the homeless shelter can't afford meat so you must serve the soup from the vegetables. Good luck and make sure time doesn't run out. Use either the mouse or the arrow keys to control your spoon that catches the vegetables. At the beginning of each soup, you will be given a recipe proportion sheet. Try to catch vegetables in relation to the proportion sheet you are given. Your current proportion and your target proportion are both shown in the bar graphs below. You also have to get enough vegetables so there is enough soup for all the hobos. The red bar to the left shows how much more vegetables are needed. You will get extra points for catching the spice containers that fall periodically.
