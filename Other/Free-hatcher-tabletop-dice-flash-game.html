Play Instructions:

Click on the buttons to choose which dice that you want a random number for. You can click to go to the individual dice to roll, or you can stay on the home screen to roll alll of them except for the custom die screen. You can click on the infinite symbol on the individual dice selection screen to setup your own custom random numbers from 001 to 999 with the up and down arrows. Then just click on the big button at the top to "roll" a number. You can use the scroll bars to view the roll history, and click on the bomb symbol to delete the roll history (it resets after 150 rolls). Click on the options button to change the background color and to mute the menu/roll sounds.
