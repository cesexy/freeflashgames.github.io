Game Description:

Choose wisely a word connecting to the adjective in this Whirled room game. The judge will decide the best answer.

Play Instructions:

Everyone is given a unique set of words. When a word is used, it is replaced with a new word. The game is played in rounds. Each round one player judges. A random adjective is chosen. Everyone else looks at their words and chooses the one that they think the judge will find most closely connects with the adjective. After everyone has submitted a word, or time has run out, the judge chooses the word that most closely connects in their opinion. The player's who word is chosen earns points. If the players do not give the judge enough choices, the judge earns points. First player to 50 wins.
