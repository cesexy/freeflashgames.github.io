Pig is stuck in jail and needs your help to break out! Can you meet the challenge and sling him to safety?

Play Instructions:

Use your mouse to play this game. Left click on Pig and hold the mouse button down as you pull Pig backwards in the sling - but don't pull too far back! Aim and let the left mouse button go when you're ready to cannonball Pig through the air, Let him sail through the air until he hits the ground. Once on the ground, left click to bounce him back up. The farther Pig goes, the more points you score!
