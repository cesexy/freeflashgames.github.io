Game Description:

The fishing version of Frogger. You are a girl standing on the shore trying to catch fish. The challenges are casting, luring without getting the wrong size fish, and reeling in without getting eaten by another fish or other sea creature. The player gets coins for catching fish and can spend those coins on new lures and rods. This is an implementation of this Fishing Girl prototyping challenge: http://lostgarden.com/2008/11/fishing-girl-game-prototyping-challenge.html

Play Instructions:

1. Click and hold to start casting, release to cast. To cast further try to release right before the rod starts moving forward again. 2. Position the lure in front of the correct size fish. Each lure can only catch a certain type of fish, if the fish is too big it will steal your lure. 3. Once you hook a fish, click to reel it in. Make sure your fish doesn't touch any other fish.
