Standard eight-ball is the second most competitive professional pool game, after nine-ball and for the last several decades ahead of straight pool. Eight-ball is played with sixteen balls: a cue ball, and fifteen object balls consisting of seven striped balls, seven solid balls and the black 8 ball.
