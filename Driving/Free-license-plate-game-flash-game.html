Play Instructions:

License Plate Game is an arcade game in which you are challenged to test your arithmetical skills. Use <- and -> keys to change lanes. Avoid other cars at all cost! To make a car disappear, calculate the sum of the digits that appear on its license plate. For example: For 26, press 8 (2+6=8) If the sum is 10 or higher, recalculate the sum of the digits. For example: For 268, press 7 (2+6+8=16; 1+6=7). Have fun!
