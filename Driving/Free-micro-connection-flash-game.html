Play Instructions:

In this game you have to "paint" all the road by driving on it with your red micro-car. When all the road is "painted" you go to the next level While doing this you must avoid other cars and cats and not hit any. For your aid there are oil-cans that you can collect and then shoot on other cars by pressing CTRL. When another car is hit by an oil-can it slides and then you can hit it out of your way for bonus points... You cannot however hit the cat.
