All the kids are too cool for school, but want to go back after a great summer to meet some new friends, but all the schools have been taken over by ZOMBIES and the kids are upset that they can't go back and have to rid the schools from Zombies anyway they can. Zombie School is a fast paced game. It's all about reaction time and fast reloading! You shoot the Zombies by placing the cross hairs and pressing the left mouse button.
