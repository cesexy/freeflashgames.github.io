Play Instructions:

The object of this game is to bust balloons to get points. How do you do that? Well, simply shoot your balloon at another balloon of the same color, and POP! They will both bust, each one giving you ten points. You could also shoot your balloon between two same colored balloons and connect them, and pop all three! The shooter automatically moves along its track when you move your mouse, and fires a balloon when you click the screen. As the balloons are popped, they will form a row coming down from the top right of the screen just so that you can see how many balloons you've busted. You will have only 5 minutes, so try to strategize your busting rate accordingly. Now you know what to do, so click "Begin Busting" to begin busting!
