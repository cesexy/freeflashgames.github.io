Game Description:

The object of the game is to blast a path through an asteroid field that your spaceship has stumbled into by using your unlimited supply of Photon Torpedoes (mouse to aim, X key or left-click to fire) and Neutron Bombs (Z key or Spacebar to launch) that destroy all asteroids in a single hit should you get swamped. You get 10 or more asteroids per second and many more of them are on a collision course. You'll need your Neutron Bombs and some rapid fire to avoid destruction!
