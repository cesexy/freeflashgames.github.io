Game Description:

In the caverns below Chateau d'If destroy the dungeon cell blocks from beneath and release your comrades.

Play Instructions:

The game is mouse controlled. Moving the mouse to the right or left moves the upturned row boat along the bottom of the screen. Use the row boat to deflect your ball towards the cell walls to destroy them and 'breakout' your friends. The left hand section of the row boat can deflect your ball to the left, the right section to the right and the middle deflects straight up. The ball bounces at random off the walls and bricks. Press the Esc key to quit the game or the letter 'N' to start a new game. Friends These little balls are behind bars in the game. Bounce your ball against them to release them. Once released, Green ball friends, can help you break more bricks and can be deflected off your row boat. However, if you miss them they can disappear beyond the bottom of the screen. Each brick is worth 22 points and a released ball friend is worth 500 points and an extra life. Blue ball friends, from level 6 onwards, can destroy the Death icons but cannot be controlled by your boat and do not destroy bricks. Slippery Moss In the later levels, some of the bricks are covered with slippery moss which causes your ball to slide rather than bounce. Death Icons and the Deadly Ooze Bouncing on the Death icons and dripping Deadly Ooze (level 7 and above), causes you to loose health, displayed at the top of the screen. Once your health is used up, you lose a life.
