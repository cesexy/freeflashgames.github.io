Game Description:

Shoot flying Easter eggs with a cannon in order for them to shatter and drop candy onto the hungry kids below.

Play Instructions:

Use the mouse to control the cannon and left click to fire. Each time an egg escapes off the screen, the kid's 'Happiness metre' (situated on the HUD at the bottom) goes down. Each time an egg is hit, the metre goes up; if the metre runs out, it's game over. There are 4 types of eggs; the smaller eggs are worth less points when shattered but can be shattered easier. The larger eggs are worth the most but take more bullets to shatter.
