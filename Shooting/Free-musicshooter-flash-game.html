Game Description:

A shooter game that you just have to shoot musical notes. It was made for the 1st Speed Flash Contest. This game is just an alpha version, I'd say a prototype, because it was made in less than three days.

Play Instructions:

Control your spaceship with the directional keys and shoot with space. Don't touch any Note Cloud, you'll lose points. The big grey notes at the bottom bar are the Special Notes. Shoot them and you'll get more points and make the multiplier bigger. Just don't shoot the red notes at the bottom bar, beside the Special Notes, those are the Wrong Notes, and you'll lose points. Shoot all the other notes, but don't shoot the same note more than 3 times in a row, that's really bad. If you play the whole C Scale (C, D, E, F, G, A, B) in a row, you'll get a LOT of points, but it's really hard. =D
