Game Description:

Inverse Invaders is a modern remake of the classic Space Invaders arcade game. The catch here is that you are not in control of the Earth defender ship, instead you control the numerous army of alien invaders! By clicking on an alien ship you order it to shoot. Each kind of invader has it's own shooting capability, so it's up to you to find out the best strategy to win the game. It's time for you to get in control of your invader army and show those Earthlings a lesson.

Controls: Click on an alien ship or an UFO to shoot. Score: Hit defender ship with top Alien shoot: 5 points. Hit defender ship with middle Alien shoot: 20 points. Hit defender ship with bottom Alien shoot: 100 points. Hit defender ship with UFO shoot: recovers 2 aliens from your army.
