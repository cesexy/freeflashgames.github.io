Game Description:

Defend your tower from aggressive capital letters by creating words from them!

Play Instructions:

The length of words must be between 3 and 8 chars. Click once to select a letter. Double-click to check the validity of your word. If it is valid then all selected letters will be destroyed. Press space to fire bullets. You have a limited number of bullets so use them very careful. For instance, fire bullets only to destroy letters which are too close to your tower. For letters destroyed by bullets you don't get any points. Please note, selected letters cannot be destroyed by bullets! To get more bullets you must create words with more than 3 letters. For instance, you get 1 bullet for words with 4 letters, 2 bullets for words with 5 letters and so on! You get points only for a valid word as a total sum of letter values multiplied by length of word. For a completed level you will get a bonus of 500 points. To complete a level you must use a certain number of letters!
