Game Description:

Efftoo is a online multiplayer game in which players take turns throwing bombs at each other!

Play Instructions:

Join a game by entering your name and wait for an opponent. Once you have an opponent players take turns in firing a bomb towards each other. When it is your turn click the mouse button to stop the aiming target direction and then a second time to select the power of your shot. Hit your opponent four times without reply to win the game. To chat to the other player, type your message and hit enter to send. Note there is a character limit for messages. If additional users join the game these will appear as spectators at the bottom of the level.
