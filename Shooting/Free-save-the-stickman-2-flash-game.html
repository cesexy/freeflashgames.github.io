Game Description:

Save the stickman from the enemies. Play till his health becomes zero.

Play Instructions:

Press the mouse, draw a line with the pencil tool from the red dot at the feet of the stickman to the door of the house, and press the play button. Press RIGHT, LEFT, arrow keys to move the fighter. Click Space bar to fire the rockets. If the rocket hits the enemy you earn ten points. If the enemy hits the stickman the health of the stickman reduces by 5%. If the health of the stickman becomes zero the game ends. If the stickman reaches the house you go to next level. Continue playing the levels till your health becomes zero.
