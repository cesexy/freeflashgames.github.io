The player must carefully aim his colored bobble at the formation of bobbles floating at the top of the screen. Matching a color will destroy the bobbles of that color. Be careful, because the bobbles are moving down. When they reach the player, the game will end. This is an original twist on the traditional color matching game. Having the colored bobbles moving in a range of different formations adds a new level of challenge to a familiar gameplay concept. The inclusion of challenge stages which requires the player to navigate through a shower of bobbles completes the game and makes it a pleasingly addictive diversion.
