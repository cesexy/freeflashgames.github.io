Game Description:

Riothub.com's version of the traditional Klondike Solitaire game. The game currently deals three cards from stock and allows unlimited redeals.

Play Instructions:

Klondike Solitaire uses one 52 card deck. Cards are dealt from the deck into the 7 tableau piles with the number of cards per pile increasing from one to seven from left to right. The top card is face up, the rest face down. The remaining cards in the deck become the stock. The object of the game is to move the four aces to the foundations as they become available and to build each foundation up in suit from Ace to King. Cards can be drawn from the stock and flipped onto the waste pile in groups of three with the top exposed card available for play onto appropriate tableau piles or foundations. Tableau piles can be built down by alternating color of descending value beginning with the King down through the 2. Stacks of exposed cards can be moved from one tableau pile to another as long as the proper sequence is maintained. the top hidden card on a tableau pile may be flipped over when all exposed cards are moved from the stack. Stacks of cards beginning with a King may be moved to open tableau piles.
