Game Description:

This easy game of ' Gaps Solitaire' is a super fun game that will make time fly! In this game you must arrange the cards so that the cards in each row are of the same suit and in ascending order from Two to King. When you discover there is a gap to the right of the card, you can move the card of the same suit.

Play Instructions:

Arrange the cards so that the cards in each row goes from 2 to K and have the same suit. When there is a gap to the right of a card, you can move the card that is of the same suit and is one point higher. When the gap is at the leftmost position, you can move a 2 to it. When you can no longer move, press the shuffle button to shuffle the cards that are not in order
