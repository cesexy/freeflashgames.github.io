ManKatha is a very simple and popular card game, mainly played in south India.It's also known as "Ullae, Veliyae" it mean "in", "out". You can play Mankatha by betting your virtual money in the game.

Play Instructions:

First the player pick a number, Fix the bet and select a slot where the card wants to be ullae(in) or veliyae(out) . Now the computer shuffles the deck starts throwing the cards on the open table in two sets, one is named as Ullae(in) and the other as Veliyae(out). If the card with number selected by player first falls in ullae(in) set then the player wins the game and he get double of the bet on the pot. But if it falls in the veliyae(out) set player will lose the bet on the table.
