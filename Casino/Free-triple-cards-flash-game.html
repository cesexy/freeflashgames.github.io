Game Description:

Triple Cards is a simple flash cards game. The game idea is to make 3 cards of the same face value.

Play Instructions:

The objective is to make 3 cards of the same face value (eg. A-A-A, K-K-K), or 3 cards with running sequence (eg. J-Q-K, 4-5-6). Player can play a card from Player Pile or Bonus Pile. Player Pile: Player has 3 cards on hand. New card will be deal to player whenever he play a card i.e. player always have 3 cards unless the deck is depleted. Bonus Pile: There are a maximum of 3 cards in Bonus Pile. Every time player get 1000 points, 1 card will be deal to Bonus Pile if there are less than 3 cards in Bonus Pile.
