Play Instructions:

It's classic battleships game. At the beginning of the game, you have to put own ships on the board, you can change their rotation before putting on the board. After placing them on the board you can always change their position, or remove the ship by moving it outside board. Between ships must be a break, minimum one field, you can't place the ships side by side. You can also put them in a random position by clicking the "random". Once you set up your ships, you can start the game by clicking "play". On the right side will be opponent board, where you can try to hit the enemy ship. When you hit his ship you get another chance to hit. The aim of the game is to sink all the enemy ships, who will do it faster, WINS THE GAME.
