Game Description:

Grow your empire to dominate the world! The Black and White empires take turns to play. At each turn, an empire chooses a colour and absorbs all the countries of that colour that touch it. The first empire to control half the map wins. The settings screen allows you to choose different map sizes, and there are three levels of computer opponent skill. You can also select two human players for hot-seat play.
