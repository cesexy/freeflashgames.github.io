Game Description:

An addictive single player card game where you have to lay down all the cards and rearrange them to get them in the right order. But beware of the free spots that can't be filled!

Play Instructions:

When you start the game all cards in a deck are distributed on the table. There are 4 extra, empy, spots. Your object is to drag and drop cards on the free spots. These cards must be the next in line with the card left of it. Free spots on the total left of the game can be filled by kings. Each card on the right position is one point. Game is over when all the free spots are next to 2's, since those spots can not be filled. The game is very strategic: it all depends on switching places in the right order.
