Game Description:

Firecrackers. Rockets. Cherry Bombs. How many lines can you form before time explodes?

Play Instructions:

Arrange the falling boxes so that they form a complete line. The shape of a falling box can be changed by hitting the space key. Lines formed that contain colored boxes will earn extra points. Be on the look-out for firecrackers, rockets and cherry bombs, as they will help you increase your points. Firecrackers blow up all of the boxes around them. Rockets blow up all of the boxes in the same row. A cherry bomb is captured when the row that it is in is removed. To set off a captured cherry bomb, hit the space bar and the bottom row will blow up.
