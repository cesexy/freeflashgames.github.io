Game Description:

Defend your apples from the onslaught of worms in this exciting tower defense game. Play Instructions:

Use the mouse and the left mouse button to build apple trees and turrets to defend them from the worms. The worms are trying to steal your apples. When the worms steal an apple from a tree, the tree will die. The game is over once all your apple trees have died. When an apple becomes red, you can click it to earn money. The longer you let the apples grow, the more money you will get. You can also upgrade turrets by clicking on them and if upgrades are available you can purchase them from the lower panel. You can also set a target for the turrets by clicking any worm. The number keys 1,2,3, etc. can be used as a shortcut for selecting turrets / upgrades. Use the Esc key to pause, the M key to toggle the music on/off, the N key to toggle the sound effects on/off, and the B key to adjust the graphics quality.
