Game Description:

Fruit Links is a timed matching game. There are 3 phases of increasing difficulty.

Play Instructions:

The objective of Fruit Links is to click on 2 matching fruits to remove them, until all the fruits are removed from the screen. The matching fruits must either be adjacent vertically or horizontally, or connected by the grey screen of previously removed fruits. There are 3 games, the first with 10 fruits, 10 the next, and 12 for the last. Points are given for all fruits removed, and a bonus for having remaining time left for each screen, more points for more time remaining.
