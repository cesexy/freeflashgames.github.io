Play Instructions:

This is the easy version of gaps solitaire card game. In this game you need to arrange the cards so that the cards in each row are of the same suit and in ascending order from 2 to K. When there is a gap to the right of a card, you can move the card of the same suit and is one point larger to the gap. When the gap is on the left, you can move a 2 to it. When all the gaps are to the Right of K`s, then you can no longer move and you need to press the shuffle button to shuffle the cards that are not in order. Enjoy...
